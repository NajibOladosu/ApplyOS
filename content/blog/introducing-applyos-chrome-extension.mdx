# Supercharge Your Job Search with the ApplyOS Chrome Extension

At ApplyOS, our mission has always been to remove the friction from job searching. From intelligent document analysis to AI-generated personalized answers, we want to help you apply *smarter*, not *harder*.

Today, we're taking a massive leap forward by bringing the power of ApplyOS directly to your browser with the **[ApplyOS Chrome Extension](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/gikepikgajfppgebbgcikhocdeejandg?utm_source=item-share-cb)**.

## Why We Built the Extension

Previously, to extract questions from a job posting, you had to copy the job URL and paste it into the ApplyOS dashboard. While this saved hours compared to manual data entry, we knew we could make the workflow even more effortless.

What if you could pull all necessary details instantly, while browsing LinkedIn, Greenhouse, Lever, or any company's career page? 

Now, you can. 

## What You Can Do

With a single click on any job posting, the Chrome extension will:

- **Extract Job Details** — Instantly pull the job title, company name, location, and description.
- **Find Application Questions** — Automatically scan the page for custom application questions and forms.
- **Sync to Your Pipeline** — Send all the extracted data straight to your ApplyOS dashboard so you can generate your AI-assisted answers immediately.

No more switching tabs. No more copy-pasting paragraphs. Just click, extract, and start applying.

## How to Install and Use It

Getting started takes less than a minute.

1. **Install from the Web Store**: Head over to the **[Chrome Web Store](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/gikepikgajfppgebbgcikhocdeejandg?utm_source=item-share-cb)** and click **"Add to Chrome"**.
2. **Pin the Extension**: For easy access, click the puzzle icon in Chrome and pin the ApplyOS logo to your toolbar.
3. **Log In**: Click the extension icon and sign in to your ApplyOS account.
4. **Start Extracting**: Go to any job posting (like LinkedIn Jobs or a company career page) and click the extension to extract the application data.

## What's Next?

The browser extension is just the beginning. We're consistently working on making it smarter and faster. In upcoming updates, look out for features like direct AI-answer generation right inside the extension window!

If you haven't yet, install the **[ApplyOS Chrome Extension](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/gikepikgajfppgebbgcikhocdeejandg?utm_source=item-share-cb)** today, and experience the quickest way to tackle complex job applications.
